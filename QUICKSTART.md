## 🆘 **Troubleshooting**

### **Error: "Loan denied"**
**Reason:** Amount exceeds your tier limit
**Fix:**
1. Check limit: `/limit` or `limit`
2. Request lower amount
3. Or improve credit: `/upgrade` or `upgrade`

Example:
```
Your tier: C
Your limit: $500
You requested: $1,000 ❌

Solution: Request $500 or less ✅
```

### **Error: "Treasury insufficient"**
**Reason:** Treasury needs more USDT
**Fix:**
1. Get treasury address: `/treasury` or `treasury`
2. Fund it from faucet:
   - https://faucet.circle.com (USDC)
   - Swap to USDT on Uniswap
3. Try again: `/approve` or `approve`

### **Error: "Wallet not found"**
**Reason:** Not registered yet
**Fix:**
1. Register: `/register` or `register`
2. Try command again

### **Error: "Database not available"**
**Reason:** MongoDB disconnected (temporary)
**Fix:**
1. Wait 60 seconds
2. Try again
3. Check `/health` or `health`

### **Question: "Auto-detection not working?"**
**Answer:**
- Auto-detection runs every 60 seconds
- Wait up to 60s after sending USDT
- Or use manual: `/repay 0xHash` or `repay 0xHash`
- Check `/health` to verify monitor is running

### **Question: "What if I can't repay on time?"**
**Answer:**
- Grace period: 12 hours after due date
- Late repayment: -3 credit points (but better than default!)
- Default: -25 points + possible blacklist
- Best: Repay on-time for +5 points! ✅

### **Question: "Do I need ETH for gas?"**
**Answer:**
**NO!** Neurvinial uses ERC-4337 Account Abstraction.
- ⚡ Paymaster pays gas
- 💰 You pay $0 in fees
- ✅ Only need USDT (no ETH required)
